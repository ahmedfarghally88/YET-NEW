OUR HISTORY

YOUR EGYPT TOURS

"Your Egypt Tours" has been Egypt's Foremost luxury travel company Since 2001, combining comfort and authenticity. We offer Egypt classic tours, day trips, shore excursions, and many other services that cater to travelers looking for high-quality and efficient services with a personal touch and care.

As a dream holiday destination, Egypt offers a wealth of experiences. From visiting the iconic pyramids of Giza and the Great Sphinx, to exploring the historic Karnak Temples in Luxor, Abu Simbel Temple, and Philae Temple in Aswan, there is truly something for everyone. Enjoy a leisurely Nile cruise or take a dip in the crystal waters of the Red Sea in Hurghada or Sharm El Sheikh. And don't forget to take home a piece of Egypt with you, be it a genuine Egyptian cotton bed sheet or a cartouche with your name written in hieroglyphics, just like the ancient kings and queens.
